# Support

## In-App Support

You can contact us directly from the HanCutE app.

**Path:** Settings → Contact Us

- Select a category and describe your issue
- You can attach images (up to 3)
- Check your inquiry status and replies at **Settings → Inquiry History**

## FAQ

Visit **Settings → FAQ** in the app for answers to common questions.
